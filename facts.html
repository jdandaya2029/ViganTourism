History

Vigan, established in the 16th century, is the best example of a well-preserved Spanish colonial Town in Asia. Its cobblestone streets, ancestral houses, and architecture, which reflect both Chinese and European culture, make it a living museum of history and culture.

Reference: https://whc.unesco.org/en/list/502/

Population

As of 2020, the population is 53,015 people. About 3,859 people higher compared to back in 2010.
